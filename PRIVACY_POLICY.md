# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** June 17, 2026

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Cherry ("the Bot", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when users interact with the Bot through Discord.

By using the Bot, you acknowledge and agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

The Bot only collects and stores information necessary to provide its features and functionality.

Depending on the commands and features used, we may collect and store:

* Discord User IDs
* Discord Guild (Server) IDs
* Discord Channel IDs
* Reminder and notification data created by users
* User preferences and settings configured through the Bot
* Command usage information required for operation, debugging, and abuse prevention
* Error logs and diagnostic information

The Bot does not intentionally collect personal information such as real names, email addresses, physical addresses, passwords, payment information, or private Discord message content unless explicitly submitted through a Bot feature.

## 3. How Information Is Used

Collected information may be used to:

* Deliver reminder and notification features
* Store user preferences and settings
* Provide Dank Memer related guides, tools, and informational features
* Improve Bot performance and reliability
* Diagnose technical issues and errors
* Prevent abuse, misuse, or malicious activity
* Maintain security and service integrity

## 4. Data Storage and Retention

Information is retained only for as long as necessary to provide Bot functionality or maintain service reliability.

User-generated reminder data may be deleted automatically after completion or expiration.

Diagnostic logs may be retained for a limited period for troubleshooting and security purposes.

Data that is no longer required may be deleted at our discretion.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade user data.

Information may only be disclosed:

* When required by applicable law
* To comply with legal obligations
* To protect the security, integrity, or rights of the Bot and its users
* To service providers necessary for hosting or operating the Bot
